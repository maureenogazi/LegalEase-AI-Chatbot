# LegalEase AI – Legal Awareness Chatbot
--
## Project Overview
LegalEase AI is a prompt-engineered chatbot developed using Microsoft Copilot to simplify legal concepts and generate legal awareness content.

Objectives
- Simplify complex legal information
- Provide basic legal guidance
- Generate awareness content
- Improve accessibility of legal knowledge

Tools Used
- Microsoft Copilot
- Prompt Engineering

Features
- Legal concept simplification
- Scenario-based legal guidance
- Content generation (Instagram, Myth vs Fact)
- Beginner-friendly explanations

Use Cases
- Law students
- General public
- Legal awareness campaigns
